5. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate data we have.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Restriction: Restrict the processing of your data.
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another organization.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where it’s the legal basis for processing.
