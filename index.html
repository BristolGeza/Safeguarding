What is Safeguarding?

Safeguarding involves protecting individuals from abuse, harm, and neglect. It’s about creating safe environments where children, elderly people, those with disabilities, and other vulnerable groups are respected and valued. Abuse can take many forms, including physical, emotional, sexual, or financial abuse, as well as neglect or exploitation. Safeguarding policies and resources are in place to help prevent these harms and to respond effectively when concerns arise.
